Working Not Working

Working Not Working is a real-time network that empowers freelance creatives to broadcast their availability to companies looking to hire them. Created by freelancers, for freelancers, Working Not Working streamlines freelancing for everyone.

We created the online presence, focusing clearly on the profile and status of the freelancer and providing a hub for hirers to have access to curated, qualified talent.
