Shipped in 2013

In 2013 we…

Launched a marketing site for Monotype’s SkyFonts service.

Shipped (literally) 1000 Symbolset specimen books to our favorite customers.

Introduced Dashboard view on Dropmark with new privacy settings, quick upload, and collection merging.

Created and shipped our first iOS app Blue during Oakweek in Los Angeles.

Designed our first piece of furniture, a 16-foot worktable made of reclaimed oak (a collaboration with Lorimer Workshop), completing our new studio space at Studiomates.

Redesigned Symbolset.com, launched while attending SXSW in Austin.

Introduced Symbolset Hosting to simplify semantic icons on the web.

Designed a fresh new packaging system for Quarterly Co.

Redefined RSS consumption with Gazette, our weekly blog-to-ebook service.

Introduced commenting, geo-location, and metadata in Dropmark.

Won 2 Webby Awards for Working Not Working.

Cooked up our second iOS app Pizza Compass with studiomate Dan Blackman and visionary Zeus Gorham Munkist, hitting #1 in the App Store Food & Drink category.

Shipped our third iOS app for School of Visual Arts, powered by the Dropmark API.

Doubled storage on Dropmark because we love our users.

Rebuilt and launched Siteleaf (our content management for web developers) to the world.

Created a marketing site for a stealth new startup, and fell in love with SVG animations along the way.

Made Dropmark better with one-click collection downloads.

Gave Unix time (seconds since epoch) some stiff competition with our very own Seconds Since Tupac.

Launched our biggest project of the year, CreativeMornings with over 345 hours of video, 11 languages, and 1,372 GitHub commits.

Hosted our first, second, and third Siteleaf Hackday in Brooklyn.

Created a new website for type foundry collective Village, introducing responsive design and web font sales.

Added developer accounts to Siteleaf, making it easy for developers to test sites for free.

Introduced Sprout, a white-glove service to help customers optimize their website and upgrade to Siteleaf.

Added 6 new Symbolsets: Gizmo, Forecast, Steedicons, Junior, Geomicons Squared, and Air.

Created Woodshop, a celebration of handcrafted wood products made in the USA.

Redesigned Working Not Working, the biggest change since its first launch in 2011.
