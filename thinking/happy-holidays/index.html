Happy holidays

There’s nothing like a roaring fire to make the season bright.

This season we transformed our studio into a cozy wonderland, complete with festive yuletide log and  IdeaPaint mantle (thanks to Mike and Alonzo for the whiteboard handiwork).

Since it’s the season of giving, we’re delighted to pass this experience on to you. Gather around your laptop, play in fullscreen, and feel the warmth. You’ll swear you’re sitting by the fire at Studiomates.

Our fireplace “mantle” also made for a perfect place to “hang” our stockings from last year. Huge thanks to Tattly magician and studiomate Bekka for making stockings for the new studiomates this year, including our very own Mike Fortress, Alonzo Felix, and Larry Fox (intern Sawyer Hollenshead was excited to make his own).

Watch or download the full HD video on Vimeo, or see a looping fullscreen version at http://oak.is/festive.

Happy holidays from your friends at Oak.

See you in 2012!
